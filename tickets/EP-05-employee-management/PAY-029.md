# [GW] Payroll - Employee External Salary and Payroll Flags

## Description

Build the payroll settings section on the employee profile where authorized users can manage payroll-specific flags: external salary (owner-only, skips all deductions and tax when enabled), MP2 enrollment, SSS WISP enrollment, and HMO deduction amount. All flag changes are tracked in the audit trail.

## Technical Implementation

### What Actions

- Update Employee Payroll Flags action: accepts the employee and validated flag data. The external salary flag requires the owner role; MP2, SSS WISP, and HMO amount can be updated by owner or payroll officer.

### Validation & DTOs

- Update Payroll Flags request: validates external salary flag (boolean, only accepted from owner), MP2 flag (boolean), SSS WISP flag (boolean), and HMO amount (numeric, non-negative).

### Routes & Controllers

- `PATCH /employees/{employee}/flags` — update payroll flags.

### Policies & Authorization

- Update payroll flags: owner and payroll officer.
- Update external salary specifically: owner only.

### Tests

- Assert owner can toggle all flags including external salary.
- Assert payroll officer can toggle MP2, SSS WISP, and HMO amount but NOT external salary.
- Assert finance and employee roles receive 403.
- Assert all flag changes are captured in the audit trail with old and new values.
- Assert HMO amount validates as non-negative.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Client-side

#### Behavior

- The employee profile page has a "Payroll Settings" section showing the payroll flags
- The External Salary toggle is visible only to the Owner role
- Toggling External Salary shows a confirmation dialog: "This will skip all deductions and tax for this employee. Continue?"
- MP2 and SSS WISP toggles are available to both Owner and Payroll Officer
- The HMO amount field allows entering a fixed monthly deduction amount

#### Validation

- HMO amount must be zero or a positive number
- An error is shown if a non-numeric value is entered for HMO amount

---

### Server-side

#### Side-effects

- Only the Owner can toggle the External Salary flag — Payroll Officers cannot change this setting
- Enabling External Salary means the employee's payroll will skip all statutory deductions and tax
- All flag changes are recorded in the audit trail with the previous and new values
- Finance and Employee roles cannot modify any payroll flags
