# [GW] Payroll - ComputePayslipAction - Net Pay and External Salary Path (Step 10)

## Description

Implement Step 10 of the 10-step payroll computation pipeline: final net pay calculation and the external salary shortcut path. This step sums all deductions, computes net pay (floored at zero), sets a warning flag if the floor is triggered, and handles the special case where employees with external salaries skip all deduction steps (3-9).

## Technical Implementation

### What Actions

- ComputePayslipAction (Step 10 segment):
  - Standard path (no external salary): sums all deductions (absent, SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, HMO, MP2, SSS WISP, cash advance, BIR tax), computes net pay as gross pay plus total bonuses minus total deductions, floors net pay at zero, and sets a net pay floored flag if the floor is triggered.
  - External salary path: Steps 3-9 are skipped entirely. Total deductions is zero. Net pay equals gross pay plus total bonuses. All individual deduction fields are set to zero.
  - For all employees: updates all computed fields on the payslip including gross pay, total bonuses, total deductions, net pay, and computed timestamp.

### Tests

- Assert net pay is computed as gross pay plus total bonuses minus total deductions.
- Assert net pay never goes below zero (floored at 0).
- Assert when net pay is floored to zero, the net pay floored flag is set on the payslip.
- Assert external salary employees skip Steps 3-9 entirely.
- Assert external salary employees have total deductions of zero and all individual deduction fields set to zero.
- Assert external salary employees have net pay equal to gross pay plus total bonuses.
- Assert no deduction line items are created for external salary employees.
- Assert all computed fields are populated on the payslip: gross pay, total bonuses, total deductions, net pay, computed timestamp.

### Edge Cases & Notes

- Deductions exceeding gross plus bonuses result in net pay floored to zero with the floored flag set to true.
- External salary with no bonuses: net pay equals gross pay.
- External salary with bonuses: net pay equals gross pay plus total bonuses.
- Zero gross pay: net pay equals total bonuses minus total deductions (floored at zero).

## Acceptance Criteria

### Client-side

#### Behavior

- Payslips where net pay was floored to zero display a visual warning (such as an orange badge or icon) to help reviewers identify anomalies

### Server-side

#### Side-effects

- Net pay never goes below zero — it is floored at zero
- When net pay is floored to zero, a flag is set on the payslip to indicate the anomaly
- External salary employees skip Steps 3 through 9 entirely, with total deductions set to zero and all individual deduction fields zeroed out
- External salary employees have net pay equal to gross pay plus total bonuses
- No deduction line items are created for external salary employees
