PayHRIS | PAYROLL App Flow Reference
Payroll Management App
Manages monthly payroll runs, salary computation, bonuses, deductions, and multi-currency support.

Core Functions

1
Payroll Run Lifecycle

Runs are created per period, computed, reviewed, and finalized in a structured 4-state pipeline.

2
Salary Computation

Supports both regular employee (10-step) and external salary (4-step) computation paths.

3
Bonus Manager

4 built-in bonus types plus custom bonuses. June and December auto-triggers built in.

4
BIR Tax

Philippine BIR withholding tax is applied automatically based on taxable income brackets.

Connected Systems

Salary Computation
Two computation paths depending on employment type.

Regular Employee — 10 Steps

1
Base Salary

Monthly rate from HRIS employment record.

2
Add Allowances

Fixed allowances attached to the employee's contract.

3
Add Bonuses

Active bonuses for the period (attendance, meal, 13th month, etc.).

4
Compute Gross Pay

Base + Allowances + Bonuses = Gross Pay.

5
Deduct SSS

Mandatory Social Security System contribution.

6
Deduct PhilHealth

Mandatory health insurance contribution.

7
Deduct Pag-IBIG

Mandatory housing fund contribution.

8
Compute Taxable Income

Gross Pay − mandatory deductions = taxable income.

9
Apply BIR Withholding Tax

Philippine BIR tax table applied to taxable income bracket.

10
Voluntary Deductions

Loans, cash advances, or other employee-agreed deductions subtracted last.

External Salary — 4 Steps

1
Fixed Contract Rate

Pre-agreed monthly fee from the external contract record.

2
Add Any Bonuses

If bonuses are attached to the external record, add them.

3
No Mandatory Deductions

External personnel are not subject to SSS/PhilHealth/Pag-IBIG deductions.

4
Net = Gross

No withholding tax. Total payout equals contracted rate plus bonuses.

Bonus Manager
Four built-in bonus types plus support for custom bonuses. June and December runs include auto-triggered bonuses.

Built-in Bonus Types

Meal Allowance
Fixed Amount
A fixed PHP amount added to every payroll run. Configured once and applies each month automatically.
Attendance Bonus
Percentage + Threshold
Applied as a % of base salary when the employee meets the minimum attendance threshold for the period.
13th Month
Auto-trigger — December
Automatically included in the December payroll run. Computed as 1/12 of total basic salary for the year per labor law.
June Special
Auto-trigger — June
Automatically included in the June payroll run as a mid-year incentive bonus. Amount configurable per employee group.
Custom Bonus: Any additional one-off or recurring bonus can be created with a name, amount (fixed or % of salary), and target period. Custom bonuses are manually attached to payroll runs by the payroll officer.

Currency Management
Payroll operates in PHP (Philippine Peso) as the base currency. Manual exchange rates are maintained for reporting and invoice equivalents.

Rate Format

# All rates expressed as:
# 1 USD = X PHP
USD → 1 USD = 56.50 PHP
EUR → 1 EUR = 61.20 PHP
SGD → 1 SGD = 41.80 PHP

Snapshot Strategy

Rate snapshot on run creation: When a payroll run is created, the current exchange rates are snapshotted and locked to that run. Rate changes after run creation do not affect in-progress runs. This ensures historical consistency for finalized payroll records.
