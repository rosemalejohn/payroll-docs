# [GW] Payroll - ComputePayslipAction - Bonus Application (Step 2)

## Description

Implement Step 2 of the 10-step payroll computation pipeline. This step evaluates all active bonuses attached to the payroll run, applies them per employee (respecting exclusions and eligibility), computes the bonus amount based on its computation type (fixed, percentage, formula, or attendance), and records individual line items. The payslip's total bonuses field is updated with the sum of all applied bonuses.

## Technical Implementation

### What Actions

- ComputePayslipAction (Step 2 segment): after Step 1 completes and gross pay is available, iterates over payroll run bonuses. For each bonus, checks if the employee is excluded via the bonus exclusions table. Computes the bonus amount based on the computation type:
  - Fixed: flat amount from the payroll run bonus record.
  - Percentage: basis multiplied by rate divided by 100, where basis is either gross pay or basic salary depending on the bonus configuration.
  - Formula (13th Month Pay): annual basic divided by 12 (monthly: monthly salary times 12 divided by 12; daily: daily rate times 261 divided by 12).
  - Formula (June Special): 100% of gross pay.
  - Attendance: checks attended days (working days minus absent days) against a threshold; awards the fixed amount if met.
- Records a bonus-type line item per applied bonus. Skips zero-amount bonuses (no line item created). Updates payslip total bonuses with the sum of all applied bonus amounts.

### Tests

- Assert fixed bonuses apply the flat amount from the payroll run bonus record.
- Assert percentage bonuses compute correctly as basis multiplied by rate divided by 100.
- Assert 13th Month Pay formula uses annual basic divided by 12.
- Assert June Special formula applies 100% of gross pay.
- Assert attendance bonuses check attended days against the threshold; employees below threshold receive nothing.
- Assert excluded employees (via bonus exclusions table) skip the bonus entirely.
- Assert a bonus-type line item is created per applied bonus with the correct code and amount.
- Assert zero-amount bonuses do not generate line items.
- Assert payslip total bonuses is updated to the sum of all applied bonus amounts.
- Assert foreign currency employees have bonuses computed on the PHP-converted gross pay.

### Edge Cases & Notes

- Bonus with zero amount or rate does not generate a line item.
- Employee excluded from all bonuses results in total bonuses of 0.
- No bonuses on the run means Step 2 completes with total bonuses of 0.
- 13th Month Pay uses 261 standard working days per year for daily-basis employees.
