# [GW] Payroll - ComputePayslipAction - HMO, Voluntary Deductions, Cash Advance (Steps 7-8)

## Description

Implement Steps 7 and 8 of the 10-step payroll computation pipeline. Step 7 handles HMO deductions. Step 8 handles voluntary deductions (MP2, SSS WISP) and cash advance recovery. These steps are skipped for employees with external salary enabled. Line items are only generated for non-zero deductions.

## Technical Implementation

### What Actions

- ComputePayslipAction (Steps 7-8 segment): all steps are skipped if the employee has external salary enabled.
  - Step 7 (HMO): uses the fixed HMO amount stored on the employee record. If the amount is zero or null, no line item is created.
  - Step 8 (Voluntary Deductions):
    - MP2 (Modified Pag-IBIG 2): applied only when the employee's MP2 flag is enabled. Uses the MP2 amount from employee compensation.
    - SSS WISP (Workers' Investment and Savings Program): applied only when the employee's SSS WISP flag is enabled. Uses the SSS WISP amount from employee compensation.
    - Cash Advance Recovery: sums the remaining balance from all active cash advances for the employee. The actual balance deduction from cash advance records happens at finalization, not at computation time.
- Updates payslip with individual deduction amounts.
- Records deduction-type line items only for non-zero amounts.

### Tests

- Assert HMO deduction uses the HMO amount from the employee record.
- Assert HMO line item is skipped when HMO amount is zero or null.
- Assert MP2 deduction is applied only when the MP2 flag is enabled on the employee.
- Assert SSS WISP deduction is applied only when the SSS WISP flag is enabled on the employee.
- Assert cash advance recovery sums the balance of all active cash advances for the employee.
- Assert cash advance line item is skipped when the total balance is zero.
- Assert line items are generated only for non-zero deduction amounts.
- Assert individual deduction amounts are stored on the payslip record.
- Assert Steps 7-8 are skipped entirely for employees with external salary enabled.

### Edge Cases & Notes

- Employee with HMO amount of zero: Step 7 is skipped, no line item.
- Employee with MP2 flag disabled: MP2 is skipped regardless of MP2 amount.
- Employee with SSS WISP flag disabled: SSS WISP is skipped regardless of amount.
- Employee with no active cash advances: cash advance total is zero, no line item.
- Employee with multiple active cash advances: all balances are summed into a single deduction.
